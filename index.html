Estimates only — lender quotes, actual parcel tax bills, and insurance quotes will vary. Selling costs default to a single flat percentage of the sale price; the optional Texas net sheet mode instead models the owner's title policy from the TDI basic premium rates effective March 1, 2026 and prorates property tax from Jan 1 to the closing date. Property tax is modeled as an effective annual rate on the purchase price; the destination-state selector fills in that state's average effective rate, which you can override. It also fills homeowners insurance with the state's average premium (NerdWallet 2026, $400k dwelling coverage) — actual quotes vary widely by location, construction, and coverage.
